Selected Works

Each piece emerges from a dialogue between refined materials, shifting light, and the intimate language of touch—where spontaneity meets intention.

The gallery presents a curated selection of original paintings and handmade ceramics, each reflecting a commitment to craftsmanship and expressive form. Works range from bold gestural abstracts to quietly contemplative still-life compositions.

New pieces are added regularly as they emerge from the studio, inviting collectors and enthusiasts to discover work that resonates with their own aesthetic journey.

Sivan Amir

Where restraint meets warmth in every brushstroke and curve

An Israeli artist whose practice spans original paintings and handmade ceramics, Sivan creates work that speaks through quiet confidence rather than visual noise. Each piece is an invitation to pause and notice the subtleties—the way light catches a glaze, the tension in a single line.

Working with natural materials and generous white space, the studio embodies a philosophy of letting art breathe. From immersive workshops to gallery-quality originals, every creation reflects a commitment to tactile beauty and thoughtful restraint.Read the full story
